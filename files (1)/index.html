Key Insights

🤑 Extreme Greed Dominates

Trades executed during Extreme Greed periods generated over $2.37M in total PnL with an 85% win rate. Q4 2024 (post-US election) was the standout period for profitability.

😱 Fear = Danger Zone

During Extreme Fear, the win rate collapsed to just 22% and traders lost a combined $117K. Longs especially underperformed — losing over $120K cumulatively when sentiment was most bearish.

⏰ Noon Is Prime Time

Trades opened around 12:00 IST averaged $243 profit — nearly 5× the all-day average of $48. Late-night trades (23:00) averaged just $36, suggesting intraday timing has significant alpha.

📈 Shorts Win in Greed

Counter-intuitively, Short positions during Greed made $450K while Longs only made $197K in the same period. This suggests experienced traders were fading local tops even during bullish sentiment.

🪙 @107 & HYPE Lead

The @107 token and HYPE were the top two performing assets — contributing $2.78M and $1.95M respectively. SOL and ETH followed. Concentration in a few assets is high.

📅 Dec & Feb Standouts

December 2024 alone generated $3M in PnL, likely driven by BTC's post-election ATH. August was the only negative month (-$106K), aligning with BTC's summer correction period.
